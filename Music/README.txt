Music Folder - ProshivkaTool
============================

This folder is for storing your music files that will be played by the integrated music player.

Supported formats:
- MP3 (.mp3)
- WAV (.wav) 
- OGG (.ogg)
- FLAC (.flac)

To add music:
1. Place your audio files in this folder
2. Click "Refresh Playlist" in the music player
3. Use the music controls to play your tracks

Note: In some environments (like Replit), audio playback may not be available, but the interface will still function normally.
