Sample Music Files for ProshivkaTool
===================================

This folder should contain your music files for the integrated player.

To use the music player:
1. Add your music files (.mp3, .wav, .ogg, .flac) to this folder
2. Click "Refresh Playlist" in the music player interface
3. Use the control buttons to play, pause, skip tracks
4. Adjust volume with the slider

Example files you can add:
- background_music.mp3
- notification_sound.wav
- ambient_sound.ogg
- custom_ringtone.flac

Note: Audio playback requires proper audio drivers and may not work in all environments (like Replit's web interface).
